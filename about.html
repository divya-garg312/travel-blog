About

Hi, I’m Divya Aggarwal, the traveler behind this blog, documenting my adventures across the diverse landscapes of India. My journey truly began with an unforgettable experience in Bir Billing, the paragliding capital of India. As I soared through the skies above the picturesque Himachal valleys, the adrenaline rush mixed with the serenity of the view gave me a new perspective—one that sparked my passion for exploring the untouched corners of this incredible country.


That feeling of freedom, gliding over lush ,green hills the wind in my face, is what inspired me to set off on a quest to uncover India’s hidden gems. From that moment on, I knew there was so much more to see, and this blog is where I share those experiences with you.


This blog began as a personal journal to document my travels and has since evolved into a place where I share tips, guides, and personal stories for fellow travelers who dream of exploring India like I do. My goal is to inspire others to step out of their comfort zones and discover the magic of this incredible country.
